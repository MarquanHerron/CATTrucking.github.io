About Us

We have been operating since 2015, starting as a trucking company and expanding to include our specialty, excavation and contracting work.

Intermediate

CAT Trucking is a small trucking and excavation business located in Winfield, WV. We deal in hauling rock, sand, dirt, asphalt, demolition, and other materials. We also primarily work providing excavation services as well as concrete services to our surrounding area.

Goals

Our goal is to provide quality services to our customers and create a legacy for our children.

Service

Some excavation services provided include yard leveling, pool area leveling, demolition of old homes and buildings, demolition and filling in of swimming pools, digging for water line, digging foundations for homes, installation of septic tanks, etc. You name it, we do it.
